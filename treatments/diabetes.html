Long-Term Diabetes Control Starts Here

Diabetes management is not a one-time consultation — it requires regular monitoring, medication review, and proactive complication prevention. At Vega Curre, we build a structured care plan tailored to your lifestyle, ensuring consistent HbA1c control and quality of life.

Our in-house laboratory offers HbA1c, fasting glucose, lipid profiles, kidney function, and thyroid panels — all with rapid reporting so your doctor can make informed decisions at the same visit.
