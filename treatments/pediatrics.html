A Child-First Approach to Healthcare

Dr. S P Kumar brings decades of experience treating children from birth through adolescence. Every consultation is unhurried, parent-inclusive, and designed to put your child at ease.

We administer all standard National Immunisation Programme vaccines as well as recommended optional vaccines. Please bring your child's vaccination record — we maintain a complete digital health record for continuity of care.

Whether it's a routine wellness check, high fever at night, or a vaccination catch-up, our paediatric team is available every evening.
