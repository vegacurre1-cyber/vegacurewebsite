Ortho Regenerative Therapy — PRP & Growth Factor

Signature non-surgical healing protocols — pioneered by Dr. Prashanthkumar

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) is a cornerstone of Regenerative Medicine. It is a breakthrough, minimally invasive treatment that harnesses the body's own concentrated growth factors to stimulate natural healing in damaged joint cartilage, tendons, and ligaments.

At Vega Curre, our Regenerative Therapy protocol is performed by an FIJR-trained surgeon using medical-grade concentration systems, ensuring maximum therapeutic density for Knee Osteoarthritis, Sports Injuries, and Chronic Tendinopathy.
