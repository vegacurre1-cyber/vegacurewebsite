Breathe Easier with Expert Respiratory Care

Respiratory conditions like asthma and COPD require ongoing specialist oversight. At Vega Curre, our pulmonology clinic offers comprehensive evaluation, inhaler technique review, trigger identification, and personalised action plans — all in one visit.

Our in-house Pulmonary Function Testing (spirometry) enables on-the-spot lung capacity assessment, supporting accurate diagnosis and real-time treatment decisions without external referrals. Emergency nebulisation is also available during clinic hours.
