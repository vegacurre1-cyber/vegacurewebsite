Our Story

Born from a Vision to Elevate Healthcare in Kempapura

Vega Curre Multispeciality Clinic was founded with a singular mission — to bring specialist-level care within easy reach of families in Hebbal Kempapura. Too often, patients had to travel far across the city for specialist consultations that could easily be handled locally.

We built this clinic to change that. By bringing together experienced specialists across Orthopedics, Dental, Pediatrics, Neurology, Gynaecology, and more, we ensure that premium healthcare is never more than minutes away — with the warmth and personal touch of a family clinic.
